# Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

## Description
Delicious chocolate chip cookies with a buttery flavor, balancing crisp edges and chewy centers.

## Prep Time
12 minutes

## Bake Time
12 minutes

## Total Time
36 minutes

## Yield
36 cookies

## Ingredients
- 2/3 cup (142g) light brown sugar, packed
- 2/3 cup (131g) granulated sugar
- 8 tablespoons (113g) unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup (92g) vegetable shortening
- 3/4 teaspoon table salt (use 1/2 teaspoon if using salted butter)
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon almond extract (optional)
- 1 teaspoon cider vinegar or white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 large egg
- 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
- 2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Lightly grease or line two baking sheets with parchment.
2. In a large bowl, combine sugars, butter, shortening, salt, vanilla and almond extracts, vinegar, and baking soda. Beat until smooth and creamy.
3. Beat in the egg until smooth. Scrape bowl sides and bottom to combine thoroughly.
4. Mix in the flour, then stir in the chocolate chips.
5. Scoop 1 1/4-inch balls of dough onto baking sheets, spacing 2 inches apart.
6. Optional: sprinkle a touch of sea salt on top for a salty-sweet flavor.
7. Bake 11 to 12 minutes until edges are chestnut brown and tops light golden.
8. Cool cookies on pans until firm enough to move without breaking. Repeat with remaining dough.
9. Store wrapped at room temperature up to 5 days; freeze for longer storage.
